ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TERM PAPER

Assignment Instructions

Objective:

Each student will collect and structure data from their respective village/community to develop a small AI chatbot that can answer questions about their community.

Research and Data Collection:

Each student is expected to gather the necessary information related to their village. You will need to research or interview local elders, leaders, or other sources to collect the following data:

  • Village Name and Meaning: Clearly identify your village.
  • History and Origin: Brief history and any significant origin story of the village.
  • Traditional Rulers: Name and title of the current and past traditional ruler with their years of reign. Igwe in council.
  • Political Leaders: Names of community leaders like Councilor, Town Union President and other leadership.
  • Cultural Practices: Notable festivals, ceremonies, or customs. Markets and market days. Religion etc.
  • Landmarks: Important places within the village (e.g., shrines, rivers, squares, historical sites).
  • Language and Expressions: Common greetings, proverbs, or local sayings.
  • Popular Figures: Names of historically significant or famous individuals from the village. Eldest man/woman in the village. Purported richest man, professors, etc.
  • Myths/Legends: A popular myth or legend from your village.

Intents and Example Questions:

You will organize the collected data into categories of intents. For each intent, provide at least 20 example questions users might ask. Each intent should cover a specific topic such as history, rulers, festivals, etc.

Example intents:

  • Traditional Ruler
  • Local Festivals
  • Historical Events

Example questions:

  • “Who is the traditional ruler of [village]?”
  • “What festival is celebrated in [village]?”

Entities:

Identify any key entities in your data. These are terms that the chatbot should recognize, such as:

  • Village names
  • Names of people or places
  • Festival names

Responses:

Provide accurate responses for each question, ensuring the information is correct and can be verified.

Data Structuring:

Organize your data in a structured format (spreadsheet or table) with the following columns:

  • Intent: The category of the question (e.g., Traditional Ruler).
  • Example Question: A sample question (e.g., “Who is the traditional ruler of Obosi?”).
  • Entities: Key information to extract from the question (e.g., “Obosi”).
  • Response: The correct answer (e.g., “The traditional ruler of Obosi is Igwe X.”).

Submission Requirements:

  • Deadline: Friday 4th October 2024
  • Format: Write your Term Paper in a Word Document that is .doc or .docx formats.
  • Submit: The structured data in a Google Sheet or Excel file.
  • Minimum Data: Provide at least 15 intents, with at least 20 example questions and corresponding responses for each intent.
  • Collaboration: You are encouraged to collect data independently, as each student will work on their own village.
  • Extra Credit: You can include additional intents (such as notable events, legends, or myths and more questions and corresponding answers) for extra credit.
